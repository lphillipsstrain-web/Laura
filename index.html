About Laura

I move through the world as a maker, a systems thinker, and a modern heathen. My practice threads together clay, steel, code, story, and ritual.

For years my work lived inside customer and technical support: listening closely, translating complexity, and building quiet structures so other people could move more easily through tangled systems. That instinct hasn’t changed, but the tools have.

I’m slowly pivoting toward cybersecurity and adjacent spaces—learning how risk, infrastructure, and human behavior tangle together. At the same time, I am deepening my path as a Völva, walking the Way of the Wyrd, paying attention to the patterns under patterns.

I’m also an autistic woman rebuilding a life after burnout. This site is a place to hold all of that at once: the mythic and the mundane, the deeply personal and the technical, the work of surviving and the work of making something beautiful.
